Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and ordering process. Can't find what you're looking for? Feel free to contact us!

What products and services does KPS offer? ▼
KPS offers a comprehensive range of printing and production services including digital printing, offset printing, large format printing, promotional products, packaging solutions, and custom design services. Visit our Products/Services page for detailed information about each offering.
How do I place an order? ▼
You can place an order by navigating to our Order/Request Quote page and filling out the form with your project details. Alternatively, you can contact us directly via phone or email, and our team will assist you in placing your order and providing a custom quote.
What is your turnaround time? ▼
Turnaround times vary depending on the complexity and volume of your project. Standard orders typically take 3-5 business days, while rush services may be available. Contact us with your specific project details for an accurate timeline.
Do you offer design services? ▼
Yes! We offer professional design services to help bring your ideas to life. Our experienced design team can work with you from concept to final print. Please contact us to discuss your design needs and receive a quote.
What file formats do you accept? ▼
We accept most common file formats including PDF, JPEG, PNG, TIFF, EPS, and AI. For best results, we recommend providing high-resolution files (300 DPI) in PDF or vector format. If you have questions about your specific file format, please reach out to our team.
What are your pricing options? ▼
Pricing depends on the specific product, quantity, materials, and services required. We offer competitive rates and volume discounts. Use our Order/Request Quote page to submit your project details, and we'll provide a customized quote for your specific needs.
Do you offer bulk orders or corporate packages? ▼
Yes, we specialize in bulk orders and offer attractive pricing for large quantities. We also provide corporate packages for promotional items and branded materials. Contact us to discuss your bulk order requirements and receive special pricing.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept multiple payment methods including credit cards, bank transfers, and checks. Payment terms may be available for established accounts. Contact our team to discuss payment options that work best for your situation.
How can I track my order? ▼
Once your order is placed, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to check the status of your order at any time. Our team is also available to provide updates upon request.
What is your quality guarantee? ▼
We guarantee high-quality work on every project. Our team performs thorough quality checks at every stage of production. If you're not satisfied with your order, please contact us within 30 days, and we'll work to make it right.
